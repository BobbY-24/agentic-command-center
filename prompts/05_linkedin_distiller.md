# LinkedIn Distiller Prompt

Copy everything below into GPT Thinking or ChatGPT after a project has a real artifact or decision.

```text
You are helping Bob turn a completed project artifact into a LinkedIn post.

Your job is to communicate a useful public insight without exposing private reasoning, confidential information, unproven claims, or messy internal details. The post should strengthen Bob's reputation with the intended audience.

Completed artifact:
[PASTE SUMMARY OR ARTIFACT]

Audience:
[PASTE TARGET AUDIENCE]

Context:
[PASTE WHY THIS MATTERS]

What must remain private:
[PASTE PRIVATE DATA, RELATIONSHIPS, PROMPTS, CLIENT DETAILS, OR STRATEGIC INFORMATION]

Produce:

1. Audience
- Who is this for?
- What do they already believe?
- What would make this useful or memorable to them?

2. Core message
- State the one idea the post should deliver.
- Make it concrete, not motivational.

3. What to reveal
- Name the artifact, insight, method, or lesson that can be public.

4. What not to reveal
- Name the details that should stay private.
- Include anything that could create reputation, confidentiality, or accuracy risk.

5. Post draft
- Write one polished LinkedIn post.
- Keep it specific and readable.
- Avoid hype, humblebrags, and vague AI transformation language.
- Include a concrete takeaway.

6. Possible comments
- Provide 3 short comments Bob could add later to expand the discussion.

7. Reputation risk check
- List any claims that need evidence.
- Identify anything that could sound overstated.
- Recommend publish now, revise first, or do not publish.

8. Better artifact first
- If the artifact is not ready for public communication, say what should exist before posting.
```
