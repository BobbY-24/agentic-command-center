# Deal Team Memo

## Team name

Use a clear name such as Bull / Builder, Bear / Skeptic, or Operator / MVP.

## Banker thesis

State the strategic argument this team believes.

## Analyst evidence summary

Summarize the evidence gathered and the uncertainty that remains.

## Recommendation

Say what Bob should do next.

## Why this could work

Name the strongest upside case.

## Why this could fail

Name the strongest failure mode.

## Assumptions

Separate strong assumptions from fragile assumptions.

## Missing data

List evidence or human context still needed.

## What would change our mind

Name the evidence that would reverse or materially revise the recommendation.

## Next action

Give the next concrete step.
