# Crude Idea Intake

## Raw idea

Write the messy version in Bob's own words.

## Why I care

State why this matters now.

## Decision I might need to make

Name the choice this idea may force.

## Context

Add relevant background, people, market, project, repo, or timing context.

## Constraints

List time, money, access, reputation, technical, or relationship constraints.

## What I already know

Separate observations, facts, and prior attempts.

## What I do not know

List uncertainties that could change the decision.

## Desired output

Describe what would be useful: plan, memo, research packet, prototype, post, repo change, or decision.
