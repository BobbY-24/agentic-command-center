# Investment Committee Decision Packet

## Decision question

State the decision the committee is resolving.

## Teams reviewed

List each banker team and its recommendation.

## Areas of agreement

Name the facts, assumptions, or actions the teams share.

## Areas of disagreement

Name the disagreements that matter to the decision.

## Strongest option

Identify the option with the best risk-adjusted case.

## Biggest risk

Name the risk most likely to make the recommendation wrong.

## Missing evidence

List only evidence that could change the decision.

## Human decision needed

State what Bob must decide as stakeholder.

## Recommended next action

Give the next operational move.
